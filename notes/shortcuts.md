* "C-c r" - google-translate-at-point-reverse;
* "C-c R" - google-translate-query-translate-reverse;

##### Move line:
* "M-P" - move-line-up;
* "M-N" - move-line-down;

##### Multiple cursors:
* "C->" - mark-next-like-this;
* "C-<" - mark-previous-like-this;
* "C-S-c C-S-c" - edit-lines;

* "M-_, C-?" -  redo changes;
